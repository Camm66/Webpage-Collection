From My Home To Yours

I was born and raised on a farm in Greece. We had our own meat, cheese, vegetables and dozens of kinds of fruit. It was hard work to run a farm like this, but those good days remain close to my heart.

My Trees, My Olives

For the past 3 decades I have been bringing my olive oil to the United States from Greece. Other oils cannot compare to the fruity, buttery and peppery taste of my own Greek oil. There is nothing else like it!

Happy Customers, Healthy Eating

My customers know the value of quality olive oil as an important ingredient in their healthy cooking. My Greek olive oil is 100% extra virgin, first cold-press, made from olives grown on trees on my family's farm in Greece.

Recipes Using My Greek Oil
